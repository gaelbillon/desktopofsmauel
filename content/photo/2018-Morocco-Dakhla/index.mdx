Dakhla is the most relaxing place I know. Lots of sport, excellent food, nothing to do all day, cozy accomodations, slow/no  internet, frapuccinos at the beach bar between two rides and a beer at sunset in the evening. Rest assured you'll get some good sleep.

Every day is wind day in Dakhla, it is as if a fan was switched on all year long. A great place to learn and practrice kiteboarding.
